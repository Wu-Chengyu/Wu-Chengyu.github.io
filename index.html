Writing Sample

Urban Talent Policies and Firms' Labor Adjustment

Writing Sample · April 2025

This paper studies how urban talent policies affect local innovation and firms' employment decisions in China. Using staggered policy adoption across cities and matched city- and firm-level data, I find that the policies increase local innovative activity and induce firms to adjust their labor demand, with stronger responses among larger firms.
